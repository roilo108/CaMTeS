Overview

The ASK modulator block diagram comprises the carrier signal generator, the binary sequence from the message signal, and the band-limited filter. The carrier generator sends a continuous high-frequency carrier. The binary sequence from the message signal makes the unipolar input either High (1) or Low(0). The band-limiting filter shapes the pulse depending on the band-limiting filter's amplitude and phase characteristics or the pulse-shaping filter. The received ASK signal is passed through a rectifier in the ASK demodulator. The rectified signal will undergo the envelope detector, a low pass filter, which outputs the envelope of the rectified signal, which is the approximation of the UPNRZ signal. The output of the envelope detector is passed on a 1-bit ADC which samples the amplitude of the envelope of the recent output signal. If the amplitude is above the given threshold, it is represented as 1; otherwise, 0.
